FLUX PRIVACY POLICY

Free 200+ File Converter
Last Updated: May 24, 2026

At FLUX, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We maintain a strict zero-data collection policy. Your files and personal data never leave your device.

1. 100% Local Computing (WebAssembly)

The core feature of FLUX is file conversion (images, videos, audio, and documents). Unlike online converters, FLUX processes your files entirely locally on your device:

  • Local Execution: We use cutting-edge WebAssembly (WASM) technologies, including ImageMagick WASM, FFmpeg WASM, and Pandoc WASM, running inside your browser's secure local sandbox.
  • No Uploads: Your files are converted entirely within your browser window. No files, file metadata, or raw document contents are ever uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • No Server Storage: Because processing is done on your local computer, we do not store, view, or retain any files you convert.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We have a strict no-tracking and no-personal data collection policy:

  • We DO NOT collect your name, email address, physical address, age, phone number, or government identifiers.
  • We DO NOT collect search history, browsing history, or keystroke logs.
  • We DO NOT collect or inspect any content, text, audio, images, or videos contained in the files you upload for conversion.

3. Transparent Outbound Connections

For full transparency, the FLUX extension only makes the following safe, outbound network requests strictly to perform non-intrusive operations:

  • IPAPI (ipapi.co): The extension makes a request to https://ipapi.co/json/ to automatically detect your approximate location (country/region). This is used strictly to pre-select your preferred language for the interface. No personal data is saved.
  • GitHub API: The About page makes a request to https://api.github.com/ to retrieve the static avatar images and profile links of open-source contributors to the project.
  • Plausible Analytics (Optional & Opt-out): FLUX utilizes Plausible Analytics on the extension page to collect completely anonymous, aggregated visitor statistics. Plausible does not use cookies, does not track individual identities, and stores no personal data. You can easily opt-out at any time in the Settings.
  • Stripe Payment Gateway: If you choose to make a voluntary donation to support the project, payment processing is handled independently by Stripe. FLUX does not see, collect, or store any of your financial, credit card, or payment details.

4. Local Storage and Cookies

To provide a smooth custom experience, we only utilize standard storage keys:

  • Settings Persistence: We use the browser's local storage (localStorage and Chrome Extension storage API) strictly to save your configuration preferences (such as conversion quality settings, audio sample rates, and light/dark theme preference).
  • No Tracking Cookies: The extension does not place any marketing or tracking cookies on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Because we collect zero personal information and process all files locally, our extension is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations globally, including COPPA and GDPR. We do not gather any data from users of any age.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect modifications in our local architecture. Any update will be immediately published on this page.
